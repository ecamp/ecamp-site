---
title: Can eCamp v3 automatically check the J+S regulations?
---

This is not possible at the moment.
This task is more complex than it seems at first sight.
Quick fixes - like simply summing certain activity durations - would only lead to more feature requests, which we couldn't prioritize highly enough at the moment.
It is possible this feature will be implemented in the future, maybe even as an external tool.
Until then, we recommend coaches filter the picasso by the relevant activity categories.
